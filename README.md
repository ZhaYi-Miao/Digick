# Digick (数刻) - Open Source Screen Time Tracker

## 🏷️ Why "Digick"?

Digick is a blend of **Digital** and **Tick**. 

- **Digital**: We track the digital bits of your life.
- **Tick**: Like a clock's tick, we value every second.

As an open-source project, **Digick** stands for transparency and efficiency. 
We believe that how you spend your time on your device shouldn't be a 
"black box." Digick provides a clear, precise, and privacy-focused "tick" 
for your digital journey.
